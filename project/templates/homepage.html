World Water Day is March 22nd — a day when the world turns its attention towards the 750 million people who lack access to safe water. But every day of the year can be a Water Day. When Rajamma opens her tap, or Grace hears raindrops filling her catchment system, or when Poppy is able to offer her children a safe drink of water — each of these is a Water Day.

Follow Water.org, and help us turn millions more days around the world into Water Days.

Water Day: Indonesia

With 250 million citizens, Indonesia is the fourth most populated country in the world. It hosts Southeast Asia's biggest economy; yet more than 37 million Indonesians lack access to safe water. Water.org recently expanded our work to include Indonesia and we are excited to transform more lives with safe water, making every day water day in Indonesia.

Visit Water.org's Facebook to view all stories and photos from our Water Day site visit to Indonesia.
